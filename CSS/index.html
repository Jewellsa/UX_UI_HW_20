"Resarch is to see what everybody has seen, and to think what nobody else has thought" -Albert Szent - Gyorgyi

We conducted 6 interview and 15 surveys to understand our research objectives which included: 1. Understand how people’s schedules affect their food habits 2. Understand if people have a tool or method to manage their diet goals/eating habits 3. Understand if and how prices affect people’s food choices.

The Problem Statement

We have observed that it is difficult for people to plan their meals to meet their weekly nutritional goals due to lack of time and organization. Eating healthy and losing weight is difficult due to the lack of a centralized database for nutritional plans, groceries, and meal plans.

Research: Survey Results

We conducted 15 surveys to understand our research objectives.

Research: I like, I wish, What If Statements

We learned that people most like knowing what they have in their fridge and what they need to buy, that people most wish they could get help with organizing their schedules of when and how long they needed to make their meals, and that people wish that an app could record everything they buy and load the nutrition information directly.

Final Prototype

At last we had our final prototype!

Throughout our testing we discovered: We still have ideas to further develop various decision points, such as giving people the option to adjust their weight loss goals or fitness goals if they aren’t happy with the plan provided or to change their meal plan. We could add more options for the fitness plan, for example. We would also like to have partnerships with local grocery stores and gyms
